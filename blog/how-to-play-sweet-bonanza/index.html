How to Play Sweet Bonanza — Beginner's Guide

New to Sweet Bonanza? This guide walks you through everything from first spin to Free Spins trigger.

🎮 Tip: Before reading — try our free demo first. Even 5 minutes of play will make this guide click faster than reading alone.

Step 1 — Set Your Bet

At the bottom of the game screen, use the bet controls (− and +) to choose your stake per spin. Sweet Bonanza accepts bets from $0.20 to $125 per spin. Start at the minimum until you understand the game.

Remember: all win multipliers are applied to your total stake. A 100× win on a $0.20 bet = $20. The same 100× win on a $1 bet = $100.

Step 2 — Choose Standard, Ante Bet, or Buy Feature

Three modes are available (Buy Feature may be restricted in some regions):

  • Standard: Pay your set bet per spin. Best RTP (96.49%). Recommended for beginners.
  • Ante Bet: Pay 25% extra per spin to roughly double scatter frequency. RTP 96.21%.
  • Buy Feature: Pay 100× your bet for instant Free Spins. RTP 94.03%. High risk, for experienced players.

Step 3 — Press Spin

Click the large Spin button (or Autoplay for continuous spins). The 6×5 grid fills with candy and fruit symbols. There is nothing to select mid-spin — just watch and let the mechanic play out.

Step 4 — Understand Cluster Wins

There are no paylines in Sweet Bonanza. You win by landing 8 or more of the same symbol anywhere on the grid. The symbol positions don't need to be adjacent in a line — they just need to be the same type.

Bigger clusters pay more. 12+ Hearts (the highest-paying symbol) return 2,500× your stake. Multiple different clusters can win simultaneously on the same spin.

Step 5 — Watch the Tumble

When a winning cluster forms, those symbols vanish and new symbols fall from above to fill the gaps. This is the tumble. If the new arrangement creates another winning cluster, it wins too — and the process repeats until no new clusters form, all at no extra cost.

In good tumble chains, you can win 5–10× from a single spin across multiple cascades.

Step 6 — Trigger Free Spins

Keep an eye out for lollipop scatter symbols. Land 4 or more scatters anywhere on the grid and you trigger 10 Free Spins plus a scatter cash pay.

During Free Spins, low-paying candy sweets are replaced by multiplier bombs (2×, 5×, 10×, 25×, 50×, 100×). Bombs that appear during a tumble are added together and applied to that tumble's win — this is where big wins happen.

Step 7 — Set Limits and Enjoy

Before you start any real-money session, decide on a loss limit and a win target. Stick to both. Sweet Bonanza's variance can be thrilling but also frustrating — going in with a plan makes the experience more enjoyable and safer.

Beginner FAQs

How do you play Sweet Bonanza?
Set your bet, press Spin, form clusters of 8+ identical symbols anywhere on the 6×5 grid. Winning symbols tumble out and new ones fall in. Land 4+ lollipop scatters for the Free Spins bonus.
What is the minimum bet?
$0.20 per spin (standard). $0.25 with Ante Bet active. Maximum is $125 per spin.
How do you win?
Form clusters of 8+ identical symbols anywhere on the grid. The tumble mechanic extends wins at no extra cost. The Free Spins bonus with stacking multipliers is where the biggest wins occur.
Is it easy to play?
Yes — no paylines to track, no complex decision screens. The main learning curve is understanding how multiplier bombs stack in Free Spins.
Does it work on mobile?
Yes. Sweet Bonanza is fully mobile-optimised on iOS and Android. Controls are touch-friendly and the grid scales to any screen size.
