RTP — Almost Identical

Sweet Bonanza's 96.49% versus Gates of Olympus's 96.50% — a difference of just 0.02 percentage points. Over 1,000 spins at $1/spin, that's a theoretical difference of $0.20 in expected return. Ignore RTP when choosing between these two; it's not a meaningful differentiator.

Max Win — Sweet Bonanza Wins Here

Sweet Bonanza's 21,175× max win dwarfs Gates of Olympus's 5,000× cap. If chasing the absolute ceiling matters to you, Sweet Bonanza has a much higher sky.

However — the Gates of Olympus 5,000× is arguably more achievable in practice. Its multiplier system rolls up cumulatively across the entire Free Spins round (not reset per tumble), making medium-large wins more accessible. Sweet Bonanza's 21,175× requires multiple enormous multiplier bombs stacking in a single tumble on a high-value symbol cluster — astronomically rare.

Bonus Round Feel

Sweet Bonanza Free Spins: Tension comes from individual tumbles where multiplier bombs might align with big clusters. Each tumble is independent — one incredible tumble followed by dead spins is common. High peaks, high troughs.

Gates of Olympus Free Spins: Multipliers accumulate across the entire bonus, so the round builds gradually. You're rooting for consistent hits that keep feeding the multiplier. Feels more like a sustained climax than isolated moments.
