Sweet Bonanza RTP Explained — What 96.49% Actually Means

Plain-English breakdown of RTP, why the Buy Feature is different, and how it all affects your sessions.

What Is RTP?

RTP stands for Return to Player. It is a percentage that represents how much money a slot returns to players over a very large number of spins — typically millions of simulated rounds.

Sweet Bonanza's RTP of 96.49% means: for every $100 wagered in total across all spins, the game returns $96.49 in winnings on average. The casino retains the remaining 3.52% as its edge.

This is a theoretical long-run average. It is not a guarantee for any individual session, day, or even month of play.

What RTP Doesn't Tell You

RTP says nothing about how results are distributed. Two slots can both have 96% RTP but feel completely different:

  • Low-volatility 96% RTP: Wins frequently, small amounts, consistent sessions
  • High-volatility 96% RTP: Long losing streaks punctuated by occasional large wins

Sweet Bonanza sits in the middle — medium volatility with a ~50% hit rate in the base game. Despite frequent small wins, your 500-spin session result is still heavily influenced by what multiplier bombs land during Free Spins, which means results vary widely. The RTP only tells you where outcomes converge over millions of spins.
