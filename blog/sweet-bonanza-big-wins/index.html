The anatomy of a massive win — what combinations are needed and how realistic they are.

The Anatomy of a Sweet Bonanza Big Win

Every big win in Sweet Bonanza follows the same recipe: high-value symbol cluster + large multiplier bomb(s) + tumble timing. Understanding each ingredient helps you appreciate both why they're thrilling and why they're rare.

Ingredient 1: High-Value Symbol Cluster

The base win comes from the symbol cluster. Grapes (2.5× for 12+ symbols) and watermelons (2.0× for 12+) are the critical pieces. A full-screen or near-full-screen grape cluster is what you want during Free Spins — it forms the win that gets multiplied.

A 12+ grape cluster in Free Spins pays 2.5× stake. Alone, that's modest. But multiply it by a 500× stacked bomb combination and you're looking at 1,250× from one tumble.

Ingredient 2: Multiplier Bombs

Bombs appear during Free Spins in place of candy sweet symbols. The key is multiple large bombs landing simultaneously. Because bombs add (not multiply), you need many of them:

Ingredient 3: Timing

The bombs must be present on screen at the same time as the large cluster win. Bombs that land on positions not involved in a winning tumble don't directly contribute to that tumble's multiplication. You need the bombs to coincide with the big cluster — and you need that combination to happen during the same tumble event.

The Max Win — 21,175×

The theoretical maximum of 21,175× requires an extraordinary scenario: the maximum possible number of 100× multiplier bombs landing simultaneously on the same tumble as a full-screen cluster of grapes. This is a certified-possible outcome by Pragmatic Play's mathematics, but the probability is vanishingly small — measured in tens of millions of spins.

The max win has been achieved. It has been captured on live streams, documented in forum posts, and verified by players. It is real. It is just extraordinarily rare.
