Is the Sweet Bonanza Buy Feature Worth It?

We run the numbers on cost, RTP, and whether it beats the Ante Bet.

What Is the Buy Feature?

The Buy Feature lets you pay 100× your current bet to instantly receive a Sweet Bonanza Free Spins round — bypassing the base game entirely. No waiting for scatters to land naturally. You pay, you spin.

The trade-off is a lower RTP of 94.03% versus 96.49% for standard play. That 2.45-percentage-point reduction is the mathematical price of convenience.

Cost per bonus for Standard includes the base game cost of ~275 spins at 1× stake. Ante Bet includes ~140 spins at 1.25× stake = ~175× total outlay per bonus. Buy Feature is a flat 100×.

The Buy Feature is actually the cheapest path to a bonus round in raw stake terms (100× vs ~175× for Ante Bet vs ~275× for standard). But it comes with the worst expected return per dollar spent.

When Does the Buy Feature Make Sense?

The Buy Feature makes sense when:

  • You have a large bankroll and want to maximise bonus frequency per session time
  • You're chasing a specific experience (big bonus) rather than maximising mathematical value
  • A casino bonus requires you to wager a specific amount and you want to clear it quickly via bonus rounds
  • Time is a constraint — Buy Feature sessions are dramatically faster than base game grind

The Buy Feature does not make sense when:

  • Your primary goal is maximising expected return (Ante Bet or Standard is better)
  • Your bankroll is limited — the Buy Feature concentrates risk
  • You enjoy the base game spin cycle

Can You Win Max on Buy Feature?

Yes. The Buy Feature delivers the identical Free Spins round as a scatter trigger. The same multiplier bomb mechanics apply. The 21,175× max win is equally achievable via Buy Feature — it changes nothing about the bonus quality, only the access method and overall RTP.
