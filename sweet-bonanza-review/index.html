Overview

Released in 2019 by Pragmatic Play, Sweet Bonanza transformed what a candy-themed slot could be. Unlike simple fruit machines, it introduced a 6×5 pay-anywhere grid where wins are determined by landing 8 or more identical symbols anywhere — no paylines, no left-to-right requirement.

The tumble mechanic removes winning symbols and drops new ones in, potentially creating chain reactions from a single spin. But the real spectacle is the Free Spins round, where random multiplier bombs land alongside symbols, with values of 2×, 5×, 10×, 25×, 50×, and 100×. Multiple multipliers in the same tumble are added together before being applied to the win — this is where the game's legendary 21,175× maximum originates.

Base Game

The base game plays out across a 6×5 grid populated with fruits and candies: grapes, watermelons, plums, apples, bananas, hearts, and candy sweets. The highest-paying symbol is the red Heart — a cluster of 12+ Hearts pays 2,500× stake. Even fruit clusters pack surprising value; 12+ Apples pay 500×.

Wins trigger the tumble: winning symbols vanish and are replaced by new ones cascading from above. If the new arrangement produces another cluster win, the tumble continues at no extra cost. Tumble chains are common in the base game but rarely produce life-changing amounts — that's what the bonus is for.

Free Spins Bonus

Trigger the Free Spins by landing 4 or more lollipop scatter symbols anywhere on the grid. You receive 10 free spins plus a direct cash pay:

  • 4 scatters → 150× cash + 10 free spins
  • 5 scatters → 250× cash + 10 free spins
  • 6 scatters → 5,000× cash + 10 free spins

During free spins, the candy sweet symbols are replaced by multiplier bombs with values of 2×, 5×, 10×, 25×, 50×, or 100×. When a tumble ends with multipliers on screen, all multipliers are summed and applied to the total win. Landing 100× + 100× in one tumble = a 200× multiplier. This stacking potential is what drives the max win.

Buy Feature & Ante Bet

The Buy Feature costs 100× your bet and instantly delivers a Free Spins round. It bypasses the base game entirely and is popular among bonus hunters. Be aware the RTP drops to 94.03% — you pay for the convenience.

The Ante Bet increases your stake by 25% in exchange for roughly doubled scatter frequency. It's a smarter mathematical choice than the Buy Feature for most players, though it still slightly reduces overall RTP to 96.21%.

Graphics & Sound

The visual presentation is bright, bold, and immediately charming. Colourful candy symbols pop against a blue sky background, and winning animations are satisfying without being overlong. The upbeat music ramps up convincingly during tumble chains and free spins, adding genuine tension when multipliers appear.

Pragmatic Play's mobile optimisation is excellent — the game scales perfectly across all device sizes, and touch controls feel responsive.
