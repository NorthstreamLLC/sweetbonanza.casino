What Is Sweet Bonanza 1000?

Released on 3rd June 2024, Sweet Bonanza 1000 is Pragmatic Play's sequel to their all-time bestselling slot. On the surface it looks identical — same 6×5 candy landscape, same cluster pay mechanics, same lollipop scatters. The fundamental upgrade lives entirely in the Free Spins round: multiplier bombs can now reach 1,000×, compared to the 100× ceiling in the original.

This single change compounds dramatically. In the original, a single 100× bomb on a big win cluster is impressive. In SB1000, multiple 1,000× bombs stacking during a long tumble chain is the mechanism behind the 25,000× max win — nearly 4,000× more per bomb than the original.

RTP Analysis

Sweet Bonanza 1000 has three certified RTP configurations. The headline 96.53% applies at most reputable casinos and is the figure you should reference when comparing games. Two lower configurations (95.52% and 94.51%) exist and some casinos may run these — always check the game's information panel before playing.

The 96.53% is notably higher than the original Sweet Bonanza's 96.49%, making SB1000 the better mathematical value of the two over an infinitely long run. The caveat: that extra return is almost entirely concentrated in rare, very large bonus wins rather than distributed across base game play.

The Bonus Round — Where SB1000 Earns Its Name

Free Spins trigger exactly as in the original: land 4 or more lollipop scatters anywhere on the grid. You receive 10 Free Spins plus a scatter cash pay. During Free Spins, 3+ scatters adds 5 more spins.

The key upgrade: multiplier bombs appear randomly during Free Spins with values ranging from 2× all the way to 1,000×. Bombs remain on screen through tumble chains on the same spin. At spin resolution, all visible bomb values are summed and applied as a single multiplier to the total win of that spin.

SB1000 also introduces Super Free Spins — a premium state where all multiplier bombs have a minimum value of 20×. This eliminates the low-value 2× and 5× bombs, dramatically raising the floor of every bomb that appears.

The trigger is harder — approximately 1 in 450 base spins versus 1 in 275–300 for the original. This is where the "medium volatility" official classification can feel surprising in practice; longer waits between bonuses are the main source of session variance.

Review FAQs

Is Sweet Bonanza 1000 better than the original?
For players who want the highest win ceiling and are comfortable with less frequent bonuses — yes. For players who value session consistency and frequent base wins, the original is the better experience.
What are the three RTPs in Sweet Bonanza 1000?
Pragmatic Play certifies three RTP levels: 96.53% (standard), 95.52%, and 94.51%. Most reputable casinos run the 96.53% version. Check the game info panel at your casino to confirm.
What is Super Free Spins in SB1000?
Super Free Spins is a premium bonus state — accessible via the 500× Buy Feature — where all multiplier bombs that appear have a minimum value of 20×. This removes the low-value 2× and 5× bombs from the pool, raising the expected value of every bomb in the round.
How often does the bonus trigger in SB1000?
Approximately 1 in 450 base game spins — about 60–65% less frequently than the original Sweet Bonanza's 1 in 275–300 average. Budget accordingly.
