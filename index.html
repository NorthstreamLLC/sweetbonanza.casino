About Sweet Bonanza

Sweet Bonanza is a 6×5 pay-anywhere slot by Pragmatic Play launched in 2019. It ditches traditional paylines in favour of a tumble mechanic — symbols that form winning clusters disappear and new ones fall to fill the gaps, potentially chaining multiple wins from a single spin.

The game's candy-and-fruit theme is immediately recognisable, and its sky-high ceiling of 21,175× your bet has made it one of the most-streamed and most-played slots in the world. Medium volatility with a ~50% hit rate keeps base game action flowing, while the Free Spins round with random multiplier bombs is where the game truly comes alive.

With a standard RTP of 96.49% and an optional Ante Bet (doubles scatter frequency for 1.25× stake cost) or Buy Feature (jump straight to Free Spins for 100× stake), Sweet Bonanza offers multiple ways to engage.

Key Features

🍬

Tumble Mechanic

Winning symbols vanish and new ones tumble down, allowing consecutive wins from a single spin with no extra cost.

🎰

Free Spins

Land 4+ lollipop scatters to trigger 10 Free Spins plus a scatter cash pay. Multiplier bombs (2x–100x) land during free spins and accumulate per tumble.

💣

Multiplier Bombs

Random multipliers (2×, 5×, 10×, 25×, 50×, 100×) appear in Free Spins. Multiple multipliers in one tumble are added together before applying.

🛒

Buy Feature

Skip the base game entirely and purchase Free Spins directly for 100× your stake. Available in most regions.

⚡

Ante Bet

Increase your bet by 25% to double the scatter frequency — a popular middle ground between standard play and the full Buy Feature.

🎯

Pay Anywhere

No paylines — any 8 or more identical symbols anywhere on the grid form a winning cluster, with bigger clusters paying more.
