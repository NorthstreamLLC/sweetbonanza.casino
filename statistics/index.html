Statistics FAQs

What is the hit frequency of Sweet Bonanza?
Approximately 1 in 2 spins (~50%) produces a win. Many wins are small relative to stake, but the hit frequency is high compared to most slots.
How often does the bonus trigger?
Approximately once every 250–300 spins in standard play. Ante Bet roughly halves this to 125–150 spins.
What % of RTP comes from Free Spins?
Approximately 70–75% of the total 96.49% RTP comes from the Free Spins bonus. The base game contributes the remaining 25–30%.
Is the RTP 96.49% guaranteed in every session?
No. 96.49% is a theoretical long-run average across millions of spins. Short sessions can produce any result. Medium volatility means results vary from session to session, though swings are less extreme than high-volatility slots.
What bankroll do you need?
At minimum bet ($0.20), a bankroll of $40–$60 (200–300 spins) is recommended. For Buy Feature play, budget for 3–5 purchases plus reserve funds.
